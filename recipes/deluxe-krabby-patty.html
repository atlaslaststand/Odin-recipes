Deluxe Krabby Patty

A spin on the classic krabby patty

This is the deluxe krabby patty. It adds more meat, more cheese, more...everything really. This sandwich will defintley satisfy even the hungriest of customers.

This sandwich is huge and we recommend not trying to swallow this sandwich whole.

Ingredients

  • Sesame seeds
  • bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Relish
  • Sea cheese
  • Sea pickles
  • Sea lettuce
  • Sea tomatoes
  • Sea onions
  • Tartar sauce
  • Secret sauce - Unexplained
  • "Chopped Love"
  • Top bun

Steps

  1. Grab bun
  2. Put ingredients on bun (basically its the same steps as the krabby patty)
  3. close bun
  4. serve to your guests
